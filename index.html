What is FQM ?

A web based management system developed for the purpose of easing the process of organizing queues and lines. Like many other (QMS)s Queue Management Systems, FQM does provide a basic dashboard to allow the users of the system and customers alike to interact with the system via a basic yet simple user interface . A point that makes FQM different than others, is the fact that's free to use and share with others, which makes it good free alternative to many expensive QMSs out-there. Moreover among many other points of differentiation between FQM and other QMS. The free base of FQM, influence the development for more freeing IT solutions, that won't require you to be more dependant on specific OS or hardware, instead finding new solutions that fits all.

Major Features

Web-based platform

It gives the ability to multiple users and devices to interact with system at the same time, without the need for Internet access. It has the ability to run on any local network .

Customization

Gives the user the ability to customize every interface , granting a unique and special experience for every user .

User management

Offers a user-based platform, so multiple users have the ability to register, log-in and monitor their own assigned by Admin tasks .

POS Printers

Support for major POS USB printers on the market. By a simple click, you can assign any connected USB printer.

Multimedia

Offers a multimedia platform, that any permitted user can upload videos, images or sound files to, so they can be incorporated in the customization process .

Verbal announcement

Supports text-to-speech clear and high-quality verbal announcement, in two different languages English and Arabic and with different preferences and settings .
